Graph Locator & Extrema Marker

Highlight a Graph in an Image

Upload a clear image of a plotted curve on a light background. The tool highlights parts of the image whose colours differ sharply from the background (50+ per channel), keeps only lines that stay connected from the left edge to the right edge (within 50 px of both sides), and ignores flat regions with no noticeable movement (10 px or less across both axes). After highlighting, the canvas is divided into 20 vertical slices with guide lines, and you can use the “Mark Extremum” button beneath the canvas to place one maximum and one minimum marker in each section—no pop-ups required. Consecutive minimum markers without any maximum between them are coloured orange to flag repeated lows.
