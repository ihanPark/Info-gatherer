Highlighting splits the canvas into guide sections proportional to the image size (about one section per 18 pixels in width and height); only the vertical sections are used to place maxima and minima. Make sure the curve stands out from the background, note that any stretch flatter than ±5 px in y across 100+ px in x will be ignored, and that perfectly horizontal or vertical lines spanning the canvas are treated as non-graph content.
